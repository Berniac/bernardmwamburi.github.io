About me

Hi, I'm Bernard 👋

A data scientist who enjoys the whole journey — from a messy raw dataset to a model in production and a chart that finally makes a stakeholder say "now I get it."

Background

I work at the intersection of statistics, engineering, and communication. My focus is building machine learning solutions that are accurate and usable — pairing solid modeling with visualizations and pipelines that let teams actually act on the results.

I care about the fundamentals: clean data, honest evaluation, and results that hold up outside a notebook. I'm equally comfortable exploring a dataset for the first time and hardening a workflow so it runs reliably at scale.

When I'm not modeling, I'm usually sharpening a new tool, reading up on the latest in ML, or turning a dashboard into something people genuinely enjoy using.
