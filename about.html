About me

Bernard Mwamburi

A Data Scientist specializing in machine learning, data engineering, and business intelligence. I build the pipelines, models, and dashboards that turn raw operational data into decisions leadership can act on.

Background

I am a Data Scientist working across the full analytics lifecycle - from data ingestion and modeling to production pipelines and executive reporting. My work centers on a simple principle: an insight only matters if someone can act on it. That belief shapes how I build, favoring reproducible pipelines, honest evaluation, and clear communication over complexity for its own sake.

I hold an M.S. in Data Science (4.0 GPA) from Maryville University of St. Louis and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Nairobi. The engineering background gives me a system-oriented approach to data problems - decomposing complex questions, modeling the moving parts, and building solutions that hold up in production rather than just in a notebook

At Gitzell Imports LLC, I lead business intelligence and e-commerce operations across two live platforms. I build Python and SQL consolidation pipelines that reconcile cost data across channels, develop predictive models for inventory forecasting, and design Power BI and Tableau dashboards that inform pricing and procurement decisions. Alongside this, I support the African Chamber of Commerce as an operations intern, where I was awarded the Emerging Young Leader Award in 2024.

My current focus is the applied side of machine learning - building production ML systems, streaming data pipelines, and the emerging class of LLMs and agentic AI applications. I'm most engaged when a project spans the full stack: real data, a real model, and a real decision at the end of it.
